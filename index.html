What We're Building

Large Conversational Models — LCoMs

A new class of AI models. Not built on prediction. Built on understanding.

❤️

Emotional Intelligence

LCoMs understand emotional state as a first-class input — not just words, but the feeling behind them. Emotion drives response, not just text probability.

🕰️

Persistent Memory States

Up to 10 years of conversational memory — recalled with emotional context and temporal relevance. The way human memory actually works.

🚫

Tokenless Architecture

We abandon the token paradigm entirely. Meaning is processed natively — 80× more economical than transformer-based alternatives.

🧬

Brain-Native Core

Inspired by biological neural systems. Concepts as graphs of meaning, not sequences of tokens. Neuroscience meets systems engineering.

Architecture

Concept graph.
Not token stream.

Meaning exists as relationships, not positions. YUDI models encode knowledge as dynamic concept graphs — the same way the brain does.

01

Concept Nodes

Each node is a unit of meaning — emotional, semantic, episodic. Not a token. A grounded, contextual concept.

02

Relational Edges

Edges encode cause, time, emotion, similarity. The model reasons over relationship structures naturally.

03

Emotional Weighting

Every concept carries an emotional valence. The model understands not just what you said, but how you felt saying it.

04

10-Year Persistent Memory

Graph state persists, evolves, and is queryable across years of interaction — without retraining or fine-tuning.
